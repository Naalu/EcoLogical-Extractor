NEW WHITE PAPER: Assessing
Metrics of Landscape Restoration
Success in CFLRP Projects 
The new ERI white paper, written by researchers
Bryce Esch and Amy Waltz, presents ﬁndings
from an assessment of landscape monitoring
plans developed by the 23
individual Collaborative Forest Landscape
Restoration Program (CFLRP) projects. The
paper, titled “Assessing Metrics of Landscape
Restoration Success in Collaborative Forest
Landscape Restoration Program Projects,”
provides a review of the monitoring
documentation of CFLRP projects to better
understand their landscape-scale monitoring
deﬁnitions, techniques and current status. The
goal was to examine the challenges and success
of collaborative monitoring and to understand the
effects of forest restoration at a landscape scale.
